# Welcome to the AI-Enabled Problem Solving Playbook

This playbook is a comprehensive guide for practitioners leveraging AI capabilities to facilitate new ways of working. **AI-Enabled Problem Solving** provides a non-linear approach to operational transformations that moves farther and faster in achieving an organization's objectives.

<Callout type="concept" title="What You'll Learn">
This course covers the complete AI-Enabled Problem Solving methodology — from diagnosing operational problems with AI-powered data analysis to sustaining improvements through structured governance.
</Callout>

## Course at a Glance

This playbook covers **7 modules** and **35 lessons** of comprehensive methodology:

- **6 Knowledge Checks** to reinforce learning throughout the course
- **Case Study:** Metro Cable — a worked example illustrating every concept
- **Exercises:** Hands-on practice with AI prompt crafting and analysis techniques
