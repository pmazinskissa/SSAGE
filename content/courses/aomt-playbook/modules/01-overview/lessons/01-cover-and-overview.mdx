# Welcome to the AI-Enabled Problem Solving Playbook

A comprehensive practitioner's playbook for leveraging AI capabilities to solve business and operational issues. **AI-Enabled Problem Solving** is a non-linear approach that moves farther and faster in achieving organizational objectives by applying data-first and AI-first principles within proven problem-solving structures.

<Callout type="concept" title="What You'll Learn">
This course covers the complete AI-Enabled Problem Solving methodology — from diagnosing operational problems with AI-powered data analysis to sustaining improvements through structured governance.
</Callout>

## Course at a Glance

This playbook covers **6 sections** of comprehensive methodology:

- **5 Knowledge Checks** to reinforce learning throughout the course
- **Case Study:** Metro Cable — a worked example illustrating every concept
- **Exercises:** Hands-on practice with AI prompt crafting and analysis techniques
