<Callout type="concept" title="Key Principle: The Problem Statement Is a Living Document">
The Problem Statement is drafted at the start of the engagement but refined throughout the diagnostic process. As data reveals unexpected patterns or stakeholders provide new context, the Problem Statement should be updated to reflect the evolving understanding.
</Callout>

## Crafting the Problem Statement with AI

AI can accelerate Problem Statement development by analyzing initial data to suggest scope boundaries and surface potential success measures. However, the practitioner must validate AI suggestions against stakeholder context and organizational realities.

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid Solution Bias">
A Problem Statement should never prescribe a solution. "We need to implement a chatbot" is a solution statement. "Customer wait times exceed industry benchmarks by 40%, driving churn" is a problem statement. Let the data guide you to solutions.
</Callout>

## Metro Cable Example

<ProblemStatementEvaluation />

<DetailsReveal summary="Metro Cable: Operational Context Behind This Problem Statement">
Metro Cable's field service division was experiencing a measurable decline in first-visit resolution rates — dropping from 78% to 62% over 12 months against an industry benchmark of ~85%. The root issue was operational: dispatch accuracy, technician-to-job matching, and parts availability were all contributing to repeat dispatches. Customers in scope included both end subscribers (awaiting service) and internal field technicians (whose workload and routing efficiency were directly affected). The 12-month time frame aligned with an upcoming PSC Rate Case hearing, creating a hard deadline for demonstrated improvement.
</DetailsReveal>

In the Metro Cable case study, the Problem Statement centered on operational inefficiency in field service dispatch. The description identified a gap between current dispatch accuracy (62%) and the industry benchmark (85%), with customers defined as both field technicians and end subscribers awaiting service.
