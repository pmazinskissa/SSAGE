Design the sustainment framework for Metro Cable's AI Skill-Match Dispatch solution after full deployment.

<Callout type="concept" title="Where You Are in the Engagement">
The AI Skill-Match Dispatch solution has completed its pilot in Zones 4, 7, and 12 with an 11-point FCR improvement. Full deployment across all 18 zones has been approved. Your job is to design the sustainment framework that keeps those gains from eroding over the next 12 months.
</Callout>

## Part 1: Identify the Drift Type

## Part 2: Assign the Right Cadence

<MultipleChoice
  question="Metro Cable's sustainment framework uses a four-level cadence: daily, weekly, monthly, and quarterly. Which metric belongs at the daily review level?"

      correct: false,
      explanation: "FCR is a lagging outcome metric — it reflects the cumulative effect of many decisions over time and is not actionable at a daily granularity. It belongs in the weekly or monthly review where trends are visible and intervention decisions can be made."

      id: "b",
      label: "AI recommendation acceptance rate by coordinator — flagging any coordinator who drops below 60% in the past 24 hours.",
      correct: true,

    },
    {
      id: "c",
