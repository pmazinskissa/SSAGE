Apply the organizational change management toolkit to Metro Cable's AI Skill-Match Dispatch full rollout.

<Callout type="concept" title="Where You Are in the Engagement">
The AI Skill-Match Dispatch solution is ready for full deployment across Metro Cable's 18 service zones. The pilot zones showed strong results, but the remaining 15 zones include skeptics, veteran coordinators with deeply ingrained habits, and a middle management layer that was not part of the pilot. The change management plan must address this reality.
</Callout>
